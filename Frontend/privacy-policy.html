Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Introduction

At Corporalis, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

  • • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, age, fitness goals, and health information you provide.
  • • Session Data: Information about your training sessions, progress, and preferences.
  • • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and interactions with our platform.

How We Use Your Information

  • • Provide and improve our fitness training services
  • • Process bookings and payments for training sessions
  • • Send service announcements and support communications
  • • Personalize your experience and provide recommendations
  • • Conduct research and analytics to improve our services

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption of sensitive data, secure servers, and restricted access controls.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • • Our coaches and staff members to provide you with training services
  • • Payment processors to handle billing and transactions
  • • Service providers who assist us in operating our website
  • • Law enforcement when required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • • Access your personal information
  • • Correct inaccurate data
  • • Request deletion of your data
  • • Opt-out of marketing communications
